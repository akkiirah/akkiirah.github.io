About Me

I'm Tobias Simon also known as akkiirah, a Game and Multimedia Developer in Schwäbisch Hall, Germany who loves creating games, graphics and videos.

Apprenticeship as Game and Multimedia developer at SRH

In 2018, after I've finished school, I went to SRH University in Stuttgart Germany to start my education on Game and Multimedia developement. This training took the form of a mainly theoretical apprenticeship. The lessions included stuff like, Web Development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and NodeJS and Vue and SQL for the backend. Also C# with the MonoGame Framework and Unity3D for Game programming. This was the time I made the two games "Speed for Needs" and "Quarx" which were created for exams and whose trailers can be found on the start page. I've also started working on a simple top down Aircraft shooter written in C# to harden my skills with that language and programming in general. This Project however was paused indefinitely due to time constraints. After two years I've graduated as "State certified Game- and Multimedia developer". I've learned alot during that time but it was time for more practical experience.
